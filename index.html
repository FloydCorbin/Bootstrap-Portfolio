About Me

I am relatively new to the world of coding, but as a former IT project manager with the Department of Technology Services at the State of Utah, I have supported and lead development on a number of applications.

My educational background starts with a bachelors in International Studies from the University of Utah. I later received my Masters in Business Administration from Westminster College. Most recently I have undertaken the 6 month Coding Bootcamp via the University of Utah's Continuing Ed program. I am CSM certified and anticipate getting my PMP certificate by the end of 2017.
